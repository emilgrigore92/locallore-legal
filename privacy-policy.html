Privacy Policy

LocalLore Trails  ·  Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Unlimited Imagination ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use the LocalLore Trails mobile application ("App"). Please read it carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account data: When you register, we collect your email address, first name, last name, and username.
  • Profile content: Any profile photo or display name you choose to upload.
  • User-generated content: Photos you attach to trail locations and audio narrations you record within the App.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location data: With your permission, we collect your precise GPS location to show nearby trails, track your progress during a trail, and personalise your experience. Location is collected while the App is in use and, if you grant the optional "always on" permission, while the App is running in the background. You may revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.
  • Device & usage data: App version, operating system, device model, crash reports, and in-app interaction events (e.g. trails started, completed, badges earned).
  • Purchase & subscription data: Subscription status and transaction identifiers processed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat.

1.3 Information from Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used only when you actively capture a photo to attach to a trail location or your profile. We do not access your camera in the background.
  • Microphone: Used only when you actively record an audio narration for a trail. We do not access your microphone in the background.
  • Photo Library: Used only when you choose to select an existing photo for your profile or a trail location. We do not scan or access any photos beyond those you explicitly select.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To display trails near your current location and personalise trail recommendations.
  • To process and manage your subscription payments and grant access to premium features.
  • To save and display your achievements, XP progress, and selected avatar.
  • To improve the App through anonymised analytics and crash diagnostics.
  • To communicate with you about your account, including support responses.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Third-Party Services

We integrate the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

Supabase (database & authentication)
We store your account data, profile information, trail progress, and user-generated content on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.
supabase.com/privacy

RevenueCat (subscription & purchase management)
RevenueCat processes your subscription status and purchase history on our behalf. They receive your App Store receipt data and an anonymised user identifier. They do not receive your name or email unless you explicitly consent.
revenuecat.com/privacy

Mapbox (maps & navigation)
Mapbox renders the interactive maps within the App. Mapbox may collect telemetry data (such as map tile requests and device information) to improve its services. You can opt out of Mapbox telemetry in your device settings.
mapbox.com/legal/privacy

Apple App Store
All in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of purchase status but do not receive your full payment card details.
apple.com/legal/privacy

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: With Supabase, RevenueCat, and Mapbox solely to operate the App as described above.
  • Legal requirements: If required by applicable law, regulation, court order, or government authority.
  • Protection of rights: To enforce our Terms of Service or protect the rights, property, or safety of us, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case we will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the App's services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes (e.g. dispute resolution, fraud prevention).

User-generated content you have uploaded (photos, audio) is deleted within 30 days of account deletion. Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Encrypted data storage via Supabase (AES-256 at rest).
  • Access controls limiting who within our organisation can access your data.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. You use the App at your own risk.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Correction
You may access and update your account information at any time through the Account section of the App.

7.2 Account Deletion
You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data by contacting us at the email address in Section 11. We will process your request within 30 days.

7.3 Location
You may disable location access at any time in your device Settings → Privacy → Location Services. Note that disabling location will limit core App functionality (finding nearby trails).

7.4 Push Notifications
You may disable push notifications at any time in your device Settings → Notifications.

7.5 GDPR Rights (EEA/UK users)
If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to: access your personal data; rectify inaccurate data; erase your data ("right to be forgotten"); restrict or object to processing; and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 11.

7.6 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact us at the address in Section 11.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the address in Section 11 and we will delete it promptly.

9. Offline Data Storage

Premium subscribers may download trail content for offline use. This downloaded content (including encrypted audio, images, and map tiles) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to third parties. Downloaded content is automatically deleted when your subscription expires or when you delete the App.
