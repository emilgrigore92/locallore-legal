Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the App and tap Sign Up on the welcome screen. Enter your email address, choose a password, and follow the on-screen steps. You'll receive a confirmation email — tap the link inside to activate your account.

The map isn't showing trails near me. What should I do?

Make sure Location Services are enabled for LocalLore Trails. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → LocalLore Trails and select While Using the App. Then reopen the App and tap Try again on the map screen.

How does the Premium subscription work?

Premium is a monthly auto-renewing subscription that unlocks premium trail content, exclusive avatars, and offline downloads. You are billed through your Apple ID. You can cancel at any time from Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device — cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

I was charged but the App still shows Free. How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Account → Restore Purchases inside the App. If the issue persists, contact us with your Apple ID email and we'll investigate.

How do I download trails for offline use? Premium

Open any trail, then tap the download icon in the top-right corner. The trail's audio, images, and map data will be saved to your device. Downloaded trails are accessible from My Trails → Offline even without an internet connection.

My offline trail disappeared. Why?

Offline trails are automatically removed if your Premium subscription expires or if you haven't opened the App in an extended period. Re-subscribe and re-download the trail to restore access.

How do I change my avatar?

Go to Account → Avatars. You'll see all avatars you've unlocked through achievements. Tap one to select it as your active avatar.

How do I unlock achievements and avatars?

Achievements are earned automatically by completing trails, reaching XP milestones, and completing special challenges (e.g. finishing a trail after dark, completing a pilgrimage trail). Each achievement unlocks a corresponding avatar set.

How do I delete my account and data?

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email us at emil.grigore92@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

The App is crashing or behaving unexpectedly. What can I try?

Try these steps in order:

  • Force-close the App and reopen it.
  • Check that you have a stable internet connection.
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the App (check the App Store for updates).
  • Restart your device.
  • If the issue persists, email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened.

I found a bug or want to suggest a feature. How do I report it?

We'd love to hear from you. Email emil.grigore92@gmail.com with a description of the bug or your idea. Include screenshots if possible — they help a lot.
